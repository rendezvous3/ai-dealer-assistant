AI Assistant Disclosure

Last updated: July 1, 2026

AI Dealer Assistant uses an experimental AI assistant to provide vehicle guidance and inventory navigation. Responses are generated automatically and may be incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated.

What this assistant can do

  • Suggest vehicles based on your stated budget, body style, use case, and priorities.
  • Summarize vehicle records, features, and public listing context.
  • Help you compare available inventory records.

What this assistant cannot do

  • Guarantee live availability, final pricing, financing terms, incentives, taxes, fees, or trade-in values.
  • Inspect a vehicle, verify legal title status, or replace a dealer, mechanic, lender, or insurance professional.
  • Guarantee that public listing or vehicle-history details have not changed after the snapshot date.

Recommended use

  • Verify price, mileage, availability, vehicle history, title, fees, and financing with the selling dealer.
  • Review the linked source listing and current vehicle-history report before purchase.
  • Have used vehicles inspected when appropriate.
This POC uses public-source inventory snapshots. Treat recommendations as shopping guidance, not a final offer to sell.
